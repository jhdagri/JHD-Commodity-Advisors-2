About Us

JHD Commodity Advisors is a specialist agricultural commodity advisory business providing market intelligence, research and broking services to trading houses, hedge funds and commercial operators across global grain, oilseed and soft commodity markets.

JD

James Day

Senior Agricultural Commodity Broker & Advisor

James brings over 14 years of experience in agricultural commodity trading and broking, spanning physical cargo procurement and derivatives broking across grains, oilseeds and soft commodities. His career includes senior roles at TP ICAP, City Group of Industries and Condor Shipping Ltd, with deep expertise in CBOT, Euronext and CME agricultural markets.

Our Approach

We combine rigorous fundamental analysis — supply and demand balances, USDA data, CFTC positioning — with technical price analysis and on-the-ground market intelligence to provide commentary and advisory services that are practical, timely and commercially relevant.

Markets Covered

  • Grains — Corn, Wheat (CBOT, HRW, Euronext Milling, MGEX Spring)
  • Oilseeds — Soybeans, Soybean Meal, Soybean Oil, Canola, Palm Oil
  • Soft Commodities — Sugar #11, Cotton
  • Macro — Energy (WTI, Brent), FX (USD, EUR, GBP), Equity indices
